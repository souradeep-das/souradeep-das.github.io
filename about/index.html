Souradeep Das is currently a Blockchain Researcher and Developer at Enya Labs (Boba Network) where he works on building and enhancing Optimistic Rollup solutions. Prior to this, he contributed to the developement of Plasma (the first popular L2 solution on Ethereum) as a Researcher at OmiseGo (later known as OMG Network). Souradeep’s passion lies in Scalability Research, where he focuses on designing solutions to make Layer 2 technologies more efficient and accessible.

Since entering the space in 2017, Souradeep considers blockchain technology and Ethereum, in particular, to be an endless source of fascination. As a result, this site is just one other channel of showing his enthusiasm and excitement to disemminate novel ideas, crypto-economic models, game theoretic constructs and more. The only necessity, he believes, is to think collectively.
